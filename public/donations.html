Kyrie Irving's Donations

Kyrie Irving’s philanthropic record is as impressive as his on-court skills. His generous donation on December 20th, where he gifted $50,000 to the GoFundMe campaign for Jaheim McMillan—a 16-year-old boy who lost his life due to police violence in Mississippi—underscores his commitment to fighting injustice.

In another remarkable act, Kyrie bolstered the education of a Howard University student, Destiny Thompson, by contributing $22,000 to her GoFundMe campaign. This donation not only exceeded her goal but also highlighted his dedication to supporting educational opportunities for students in Historically Black Colleges and Universities (HBCUs).
